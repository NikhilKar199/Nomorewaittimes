About Us

This website, No More Wait Times, was created by Nikhil Kar with the guidance and support of my project mentor, Noam Borgnia.

The purpose of this website is to help theme park visitors better plan their trips by avoiding long ride wait times and ensuring a smooth, enjoyable experience. With features such as customized trip planning, ride availability tracking, and time validation, this site is designed to make your theme park experience more efficient and fun.

Thank you for using our service. We hope it makes your next trip more enjoyable!
